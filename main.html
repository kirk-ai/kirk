Celebrating World Literacy Day

Unlocking Worlds, One Page at a Time

At St Dominic's High School, we believe that literacy is the key to a brighter future. On World Literacy Day, we come together to celebrate the power of reading, writing, and learning. Join us as we inspire a love for books and empower every student to become a lifelong learner!

World Literacy Day Activities

Mobile Journalism Contest

Use your smartphone or tablet to capture and report real stories happening around you. Learn how to research, interview, take photos, and create news articles or videos using digital tools. Mobile journalism helps you develop digital literacy skills by using technology to share information, tell stories, and make your voice heard in the digital world!

Digital Story Telling Challenge

Use your creativity and digital skills to craft a story using text, images, audio, or video. Show how digital tools can bring your ideas to life and help you share your voice with the world.
